The Challenge
What Uganda's Farmers & Vendors Face
These are the real problems we set out to solve — documented through profiling over 2,700 farmers and 400+ vendors across Uganda.
📉

Price Fluctuations

Farmers lose up to 60% of value during glut seasons with no mechanism to lock prices ahead of harvest.

🔗

Too Many Middlemen

Produce passes through 4–6 hands before reaching consumers. Farmers earn only 15–25% of final price.

📵

Limited Market Access

Vendors have no reliable way to find and contact farmers for fresh, consistent produce in their area.

📦

Inconsistent Supply

Hotels and restaurants cannot plan menus because supply is unreliable, causing food waste and revenue loss.

📚

Limited Information

Farmers in deep villages have no access to market prices, best practices or training resources.

⏰

Timing Mismatch

Produce spoils because buyers and sellers can't coordinate harvest timing and delivery schedules.

🌾

Poor Post-Harvest

Up to 40% of produce is lost post-harvest due to lack of storage knowledge, packaging and cold chain.

💳

No Credit Access

Farmers cannot access input finance because they have no credit history or digital financial profile.

🔒 Forward Contract System — How We Stabilise Prices

Farmers and buyers can lock a price 4–8 weeks before harvest. The platform holds escrow, guarantees delivery and releases payment upon quality-verified delivery. This eliminates the price shock of seasonal gluts and protects farmers' income.

End-to-End Profiling
Every Stakeholder, Fully Profiled
We profile farmers, vendors, hotels and buyers in depth so the platform can serve each person's exact needs.

🌱 Farmer Profile

A 4-step registration builds a complete digital identity for every farmer — enabling matching, training, credit scoring and fair pricing.

  • National ID verification
  • GPS farm location mapping
  • Land size & ownership type
  • Crop list & monthly output (kg)
  • Irrigation & storage facilities
  • MTN / Airtel Mobile Money account
  • Planting & harvest calendar
  • Experience years & training history
  • Credit history & financial challenges
  • Language preference (Luganda/English/Swahili)

🏪 Vendor Profile

Market vendors are profiled by market, product categories and buying volumes so farmers can find the right buyers instantly.

  • Business permit / KCCA number
  • Market name & GPS stall location
  • Product categories (vegetables, fruits, grains)
  • Weekly buying volume (kg)
  • Buyer types (retail, wholesale, export)
  • Quality grade requirements (A/B/C)
  • Payment terms (cash, mobile money, credit)
