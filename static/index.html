How do I register as a farmer? ▼
Click "Join Free" in the top right, fill in your name, phone and district — under 2 minutes. Or dial *789# from any MTN or Airtel phone.
Can I sell both crops and animals? ▼
Yes! AgriBridge supports both. List your crops on the Marketplace and your livestock, poultry, fish, or honey on the Animals page. Both are free to list.
Is AgriBridge free for farmers? ▼
Yes — listing your produce or animals is always free. We charge a small 2.5% commission only when a transaction is completed successfully.
How does the *789# USSD work? ▼
Dial *789# from any MTN or Airtel phone. No internet needed. You can check crop prices, animal prices, list produce, find buyers, and get farming tips — in English or Luganda.
How does payment work? ▼
Buyers pay via MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money. Funds are held securely until delivery is confirmed, then released to the farmer. No bank account needed.
How does the AI Animal Doctor work? ▼
Go to the Animals page → scroll down to AI Animal Doctor. Select your animal type and symptom, and the AI gives instant diagnosis with treatment and prevention advice. It covers ECF, FMD, Newcastle, Mastitis, ASF, and more. Book a real vet visit directly from the same page.
Do animals need health certificates? ▼
Health certificates are optional but recommended. Certified animals command higher prices. AgriBridge helps connect you with licensed vets who can issue MAAIF-approved certificates. Animals marked 🏥 have health certs available.

Common Uganda Animal Diseases
🦠
East Coast Fever (ECF)
Animals: Cattle · Symptoms: High fever 40°C+, swollen lymph nodes, breathing difficulty · Cause: Theileria parva (tick-borne)
Treatment: Buparvaquone (Butalex) injection ASAP. Tick dipping with Amitraz. Immunise susceptible herds (ITM method).
🐄
Foot & Mouth Disease (FMD)
Animals: Cattle, Goats, Pigs · Symptoms: Blisters on mouth & feet, lameness, reduced milk · Cause: FMD Virus (notifiable disease)
REPORT to DVO immediately. Isolate animals. Citric acid disinfection. Annual MAAIF FMD vaccine campaign.
🐔
Newcastle Disease (NCD)
Animals: Chickens, Ducks · Symptoms: Sudden death, gasping, twisted neck, green diarrhea · Cause: Paramyxovirus (highly contagious)
Vaccinate day-old (Komarov/V4 strain). No treatment — report to DVO. Weekly disinfection. Biosecurity lockdown.
🐖
African Swine Fever (ASF)
Animals: Pigs · Symptoms: Hemorrhagic fever, red skin blotches, sudden death · Cause: ASF Virus (no vaccine)
CRITICAL: Cull & bury affected pigs. Report immediately to DVO. Do NOT sell sick pigs — criminal offence.
🐐
Contagious Caprine Pleuropneumonia
Animals: Goats · Symptoms: Severe pneumonia, nasal discharge, respiratory distress · Cause: Mycoplasma capricolum
Tylosin injection (10mg/kg) for 5 days. Vaccinate annually. Quarantine new goats 21 days. Reduce crowding.
🐟
Bacterial Fish Disease (BFD)
Animals: Tilapia, Catfish · Symptoms: Ulcers, hemorrhagic fins, erratic swimming · Cause: Aeromonas, Streptococcus
Reduce stocking density. Salt baths (15g/L, 15 min). Oxytetracycline feed 10 days. Test water quality weekly.
🐂
Brucellosis (Bang's Disease)
Animals: Cattle, Goats · Symptoms: Abortion storms, retained placenta, infertility in females · Cause: Brucella bacteria (ZOONOTIC)
Vaccinate females before first calving (RB51). Test-and-cull policy. WEAR GLOVES — Brucellosis infects humans.
🐛
Internal Parasites (Helminths)
Animals: All livestock · Symptoms: Weight loss, pot belly, diarrhea, bottle jaw, anaemia · Cause: Roundworm, tapeworm, liver fluke
Deworm quarterly (Albendazole or Ivermectin). Rotate pastures. Fecal egg count test before treatment. Kids monthly.
